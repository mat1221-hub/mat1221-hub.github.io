About Me

Welcome to my professional website! My name is Mulat Ayinet Tiruye, and I am currently pursuing a Master's degree in Computer Engineering at the University of Pisa, Italy. I am a dedicated and highly motivated professional actively seeking opportunities for employment, internships, or further studies. My interests and expertise span computer architecture, photonic computing, quantum computing, cloud computing, parallel and distributing computing, high-performance computing, artificial intelligence (AI), machine learning (ML), VLSI design (digital, analog, and mixed-signal IC design), and hardware acceleration for ML and AI, as well as other areas related to electrical, nanoelectronics, and computer engineering.

My academic journey is marked by a commitment to excellence and a passion for cutting-edge technology. I hold dual Master's degrees in Nano Electronics Engineering and Design from both Chang Gung University, Taiwan, and Singapore University of Technology and Design. Prior to this, I completed my Bachelor's degrees at Kalinga Institute of Industrial Technology (KIIT) University in India, where I laid the foundation for my academic pursuits.
My research interests span a broad spectrum of advanced topics in technology. I am particularly fascinated by computer architecture, exploring novel paradigms such as photonic computing and quantum computing. My expertise extends to cloud computing, parallel and distributed computing, and high-performance computing, where I strive to push the boundaries of what is possible.
Artificial intelligence (AI) and machine learning (ML) are integral to my research endeavors, where I focus on developing innovative solutions and algorithms. Additionally, my proficiency in VLSI design encompasses digital, analog, and mixed-signal IC design, complemented by hardware acceleration techniques tailored for ML and AI applications.
Driven by a self-motivated and research-oriented mindset, I am dedicated to contributing meaningfully to the field of computer engineering. My goal is to leverage my diverse academic background and technical skills to address complex challenges and drive innovation forward.
